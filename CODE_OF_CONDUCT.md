# Code of Conduct

Mara public repositories exist so people can build, test, learn, and improve useful software
together. Keep participation professional, specific, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but correct problems directly.
- Keep feedback about the work, not the person.
- Respect different backgrounds, languages, skill levels, and time zones.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce or understand the issue.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexual, violent, discriminatory, or demeaning language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, repeated off-topic comments, or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Pushing secrets, real personal data, or private customer data into public repositories.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; block users; or take
other moderation steps when needed to keep the project usable.

To report conduct concerns, contact `contact@iammara.com` with links, screenshots, and relevant
context.
